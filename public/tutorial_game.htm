Tutorial 2/2

Welcome to the rounds of game tutorial. As in the rounds of training, you will have to classify the five dogs on the screen, but now there will be four categories: the two in which you were trained and the two in which your partner was trained.

Additionally, you will see two new elements on the screen: the Pending Messages list and the Question Mark . During the game, you can use these elements to interact with your partner. In this tutorial you will learn how to use these tools.

Please pay attention to the Instructions Box in the bottom left of your screen to advance in this tutorial.

N.B.: the dogs that you will see in this tutorial are not the same that you will classify during the test.
