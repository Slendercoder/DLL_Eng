Score and times

Recall that:

  • There are 20 rounds of training and 25 rounds of game.
  • Your score on each round depends only on your choices.
  • During each round of training you will have 60 seconds to classify the dogs.
  • During each round of game you will have 80 seconds to classify the dogs.
  • During each round, you will have 20 seconds to see the feedback from your classification.

If you understand this information, please press "Continue".
