Tutorial 1/2

Welcome to the rounds of training tutorial. Your goal is to learn to classify the dogs that you see in the screen. There are two categories of dogs. You can see them by clicking on the lists below each image.

Please pay attention to the Instruction Box in the bottom left of your screen to advance in the tutorial.

N.B.: the dogs that you will see in this tutorial are not the same that you will classify in this test

Classify the dogs

During each round of training, you should classify dogs from two categories. By cliking the list below each image, you can make a choice between two categories. During the game, you can press the "Confirm" button to end the round.

However, to continue this tutorial, you should classify all the five dogs to enable the "Confirm" button.
