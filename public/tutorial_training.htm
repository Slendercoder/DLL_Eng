Tutorial 1/2

Welcome to the rounds of training tutorial. Your goal during these rounds will be to learn to classify dogs that are drawn from two categories. You will receive feedback at the end of each round to guide you in your learning process.

To assign each dog a category, just click on the list below the respective image.

Please pay attention to the Instructions Box in the bottom left of your screen to advance in the tutorial.

N.B.: the dogs that you will see in this tutorial are not the same as you will classify during the test.

Classify the dogs

During each round of training, you should classify dogs from two categories. By cliking the list below each image, you can make a choice between two categories. During the game, you can press the "Confirm" button to end the round.

However, to continue this tutorial, you should classify all the five dogs to enable the "Confirm" button.
